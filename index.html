About ALOREM

ALOREM (Vincenzo Merola) is a talented musician, born in Battipaglia (Salerno) in February 1996. He has been playing the violin since the age of four and holds two degrees from the conservatory. Over the years, he has performed at numerous events and venues across Italy.

He is a versatile artist: he plays both classical and electric violin, he performs as a DJ, and he is also a singer-songwriter producing his own music. He masterfully blends classical techniques with modern sounds, creating a unique musical style.

Currently, he is collaborating with several venues in Milan, performing electric violin sets over contemporary commercial tracks, captivating audiences with his innovative fusion of tradition and modernity.
